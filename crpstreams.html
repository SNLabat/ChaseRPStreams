⚡ Quick Setup

To display live streams, you need to configure your Twitch API credentials:

  1. Go to the Twitch Developer Console
  2. Log in with your Twitch account
  3. Click "Register Your Application"
  4. Fill in the details:
    • Name: Chase RP Stream Viewer (or any name)
    • OAuth Redirect URLs: http://localhost
    • Category: Website Integration
  5. Click "Create", then click "Manage" on your new app
  6. Copy your Client ID and generate a new Client Secret
  7. Edit this HTML file and update the config below:
// Find this near the top of the <script> section and replace with your values

CLIENT_ID: 'your_client_id_here',
CLIENT_SECRET: 'your_client_secret_here',
